The Emotional Burden of Being Hacked

Experiencing a hack can be deeply unsettling. Victims often feel exposed, anxious, and violated, with emotional consequences that last long after the event.

Many describe feelings of stress and powerlessness, especially when they do not know which data has been accessed or how it has been shared.

Delayed breach notifications often worsen the emotional impact, leaving victims confused and uncertain about how to regain control of their digital lives.

Even after accounts are restored, many struggle with trust online, feeling vulnerable and cautious in their digital interactions.
